Who We Are

Spectacular Cleaning Services is a London-based cleaning company founded by two sisters with a shared passion for excellence and over a decade of experience in the cleaning and construction industries. We are fully insured, health & safety trained, and known for reliable, professional results across residential and commercial spaces.

Our Services

Post-Construction Cleaning

Specialist cleaning for newly built or renovated spaces. We remove dust, debris, and residue to get your site ready for handover or staging.

Deep Cleaning

A comprehensive top-to-bottom clean perfect for move-ins, move-outs, seasonal resets, or annual maintenance. Every room and surface is refreshed.

Office Cleaning

Tailored cleaning schedules to suit your business needs. Includes desks, meeting rooms, kitchens, and washrooms for a spotless workspace.

End-of-Tenancy Cleaning

Ensure properties meet agency and landlord expectations. Includes oven cleaning, limescale removal, and carpet vacuuming or shampooing.

Post-Event Cleaning

We clean up after parties, weddings, and corporate events. From rubbish removal to stain treatment—even late nights and weekends.

Domestic Cleaning

Routine cleaning services for busy households. Choose from weekly, fortnightly or monthly plans with consistent, trusted cleaners.

Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Deep cleaning for carpets, rugs, sofas, and more—removing stains, odours, and allergens with professional-grade equipment.
